Who We Are

We are a group of students from Hwa Chong Institution who aim to raise awareness and funds for our beneficiary, Diabetes Singapore.

Hey everyone, we are Project Vitalize, an aspiring Youth For Causes (YFC) project and we aim to raise awareness and funds for the diabetic community in Singapore, and also educate the public more about the rising threat of diabetes. With your support, we will be able to carry out our events and initiatives in mind for 2019, such as concerts, walks, street sales, and much more!
